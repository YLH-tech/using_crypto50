Installation Guide

Android Installation

Step 1: Scan the QR code to begin the APK download.

Step 2(Optional): For security, your phone is set to block the installation of apps from unknown sources. To proceed, tap Settings, and enable "Allow from this source."

Step 3 (Optional): Allow installation from unknown sources by toggling the setting in Security.

Step 4: Open the APK file you downloaded. Tap Install and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation process.

Step 5: Once installation is complete, tap Finish to complete the setup. You can now start using the app!

iOS Installation

Step 1: Scan the QR code and open it in Safari browser.

Step 2: Tap the Share button in Safari to open the share options.

Step 3: From the share options, tap Add to Home Screen.

Step 4: Safari will show a new page asking for confirmation. Tap the Add button to confirm.

Step 5: The app icon will now appear on your home screen. Tap the icon to open the app.
